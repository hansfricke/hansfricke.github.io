Hans Fricke

I am the Director of Quantitative Research of the PACE-CORE Research Partnership. Moreover, I am a Research Fellow at the Global Labor Organization and a Research Affiliate at the IZA Institute of Labor Economics and a volunteer data scientist at DataKind.

Prior to this position I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Stanford University Center for Education Policy Analysis (CEPA) and the Director of Research of CEPA Labs. I received his Master’s (Diplom Univ.) in International Economics from the Friedrich-Alexander University Erlangen-Nürnberg in 2011 and my Ph.D. in Economics and Finance from the University of St. Gallen in 2016. My research interest focus on education policy, behavioral interventions in education, and causal analysis.
